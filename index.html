About

Hi, I'm Chou, Ming-Kun — a kid from Linbian with a little music and a little sports under my belt. I share my home in Tainan with a dachshund named Fries (薯條). I study in the Cross College Elite Program at NCKU, majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and I've been lucky to meet remarkable people across many fields. I care less about how deep the tech goes than what a project is really worth beyond the problem itself; I like moving plans and research forward through design thinking, and I enjoy exploring and discovering along the way. These days I'm collaborating with several labs on some genuinely cool work.
